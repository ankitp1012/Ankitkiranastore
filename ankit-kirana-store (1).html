FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order on WhatsApp?

Simply save our number (+91 9691821012) and send us a WhatsApp message with your list of required products. We'll confirm availability, share the total, and arrange delivery or pickup as per your preference.

Do you provide home delivery?

Yes! We provide home delivery within Muraria and nearby areas. For bulk orders and monthly packs, delivery is free. For smaller orders, a nominal delivery charge may apply based on location.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), and bank transfers. We're working to make payments even easier for our customers.

What are the store timings?

Our physical store is open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday to Sunday. WhatsApp orders are accepted 24/7 and will be processed during store hours the same or next day.

Can I order monthly grocery packs?

Absolutely! Our Premium Monthly Pack is perfect for families who want convenience. Order once and we'll make sure you never run out of essentials. You get priority service and exclusive discounts on monthly subscriptions.
